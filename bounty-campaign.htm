A​ ​Stake​ ​System :

The Bounty Campaign will operate as a stake system.

Please see an example of how stake system works:

  • At the end of the Bounty Campaign there are 100 people participating in the Bounty Campaign.
  • At the end these 100 people claimed a total of 200 stakes.
  • In this example the Bounty Campaign has totally 200,000 EMI allocated to it.

User A finishes 2 translations, and owns 2 stakes.

User B finishes 6 translations, and owns 6 stakes.

This means a total of 8 stakes will be distributed to User A and User B.

User A will receive 1% (2/200) of the 200,000 EMI, i.e. 2,000 EMI.

User B will receive 3% (6/200) of the 200,000 EMI, i.e. 6,000 EMI.
